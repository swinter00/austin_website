Music

Austin Muthyala is a versatile musician that can blend into a variety of musical styles. At 21 years old, as a member of MSU Jazz Orchestra he had the opportunity to perform with many of todayâ€™s celebrated musicians such as: Terell Stafford, Melissa Aldana, Harvey Mason, Tim Warfield, and Kenny Washington. He is a Geri Allen fellow and a member of the Gathering Orchestra under the direction of Rodney Whitaker. In addition, professionally, he is a core member of Orquesta RITMO, one of the premier salsa bands in West Michigan, as well as Global Village, SLOAN and the Filthy Animals, and the BlueWater Kings organization

Austin is currently in his senior year at Michigan State University, where he is pursuing a Bachelor of Music in Jazz Studies. There, he currently studies with Etienne Charles and performs in ensembles under the direction of Rodney Whitaker. In the past, Austin has studied with Alphonso Horne, Marcus Printup, Max Colley III, and others.
