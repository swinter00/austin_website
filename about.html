    About Austin Muthyala

    Austin Muthyala is a young and upcoming jazz trumpet player from Byron Center, Michigan. He has twelve years of experience with his instrument. Austin has performed throughout the world, including in Japan, New York City, and across Michigan. Currently, he is an active member of the local music scene in Lansing, MI.

    Most recently, Austin has begun the path of content creation by creating his YouTube channel, muthybaby. Inspired by his favorite content creators (YouTubers and streamers alike), he decided to take the plunge and use the new platform as another means of sharing his music.

    When he is not playing music, you can find Austin gaming with his friends, editing videos, watching anime, or reading.
