About Austin Muthyala

Austin Muthyala is a young and upcoming jazz trumpet player from Byron Center, Michigan. He is a versatile musician that can blend into a variety of musical styles. At 21 years old, as a member of MSU Jazz Orchestra he had the opportunity to perform with many of todayâ€™s celebrated musicians such as: Terell Stafford, Melissa Aldana, Harvey Mason, Tim Warfield, and Kenny Washington. He is a Geri Allen fellow and a member of the Gathering Orchestra under the direction of Rodney Whitaker.

In addition, professionally, he is a core member of Orquesta RITMO, one of the premier salsa bands in West Michigan, as well as Global Village, SLOAN and the Filthy Animals, and the BlueWater Kings organization. He is a student of Etienne Charles in his senior year at Michigan State University. Currently, he is a member of the local music scene in Lansing, MI.

Most recently, Austin has begun the path of content creation by creating his YouTube channel, muthybaby. Inspired by his favorite content creators (YouTubers and streamers alike), he decided to take the plunge and use the new platform as another means of sharing his music.

When he is not playing music, you can find Austin gaming with his friends, editing videos, watching anime, or reading.
